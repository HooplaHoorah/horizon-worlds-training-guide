# Meta Horizon Creator Competition: Mobile Innovation – Contest Brief

## High-Level

- Host: Meta + DevPost (Meta Horizon Creator Program)
- Theme: **Mobile Innovation** in Horizon Worlds
- Goal: Build a new world/game/experience that fully leverages mobile capabilities in Horizon Worlds.

## Schedule (2025)

- Entry window: **Nov 3 – Dec 8 @ 5:00 PM PST**
- Community voting: **Dec 10 – Dec 12**
- Judging: **Dec 9 – Dec 16**
- Winners announced: From **Dec 22**  

## Eligibility (short version)

- Must be in the **Meta Horizon Creator Program (MHCP)**.
- Age 18+ and in a supported country.
- Have a published world on Horizon.
- Can enter solo or as a team (2–10 people).

## What We Must Build

- A **new, original Horizon Worlds world**, created with the **desktop editor**.
- Designed to run **smoothly on mobile** and feel mobile-first (controls, UX, etc.).
- Uses only publicly available / MHCP-available tools & features.
- Strongly encouraged to use at least **one GenAI feature** (e.g., AI-assisted building/tools).

## Technical & Content Requirements (summary)

- Original IP (or public-domain / Meta-provided content).
- Follows Meta & MHCP policies (Community Standards, content guidelines, etc.).
- World is **published** and **publicly accessible** in Horizon.
- Optimized for mobile performance (poly counts, effects, UI legibility, etc.).

## Required Submission Deliverables (DevPost)

1. World name  
2. URL to the published world  
3. Publisher’s Meta Horizon username (main contact)  
4. Team name  
5. Team members + their roles (scripting, art, design, etc.)  
6. Recommended number of players (1–2 or 3+)  
7. 200–300 word write-up covering:
   - Concept & inspiration
   - How it was built
   - Future improvement ideas
8. One **demo video** (<= 3 minutes):
   - Shows the experience running on **mobile**
   - Publicly hosted (YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook Video, or Youku)
   - Linked in the DevPost submission
   - No unlicensed copyrighted music or IP

> **Repo:** Not required by the rules but we will keep a `GSI-HQ` Git repo with:
> - Design docs
> - Planning notes
> - Any supporting code/scripts
> - Assets lists

## Judging Criteria (what we must optimize for)

1. **Technical Innovation**
   - Smart use of mobile device features (touch, orientation, haptics, etc.)
   - Solid, reliable scripting and systems design.

2. **Visuals & Sound**
   - Art, UI, and audio that look/sound good on a phone screen.
   - Readable UI, clean composition, and performant visuals.

3. **Mobile Experience Design**
   - Mobile-first onboarding and controls.
   - Clear goals within seconds of spawning.
   - Short, satisfying loops that work in bite-sized sessions.

4. **Social Connectivity**
   - Fun with friends or strangers.
   - Moments worth sharing / replaying.
   - Mechanics that feel natural in mobile social play.

## Design Target for GSI-HQ

Working theme for our world:
- **GSI-HQ: Glam Scene Investigation** – a stylish, mobile-friendly detective HQ where players solve “glam crimes” via quick investigative loops, co-op mini-missions, and social deduction moments.
- Must showcase:
  - Strong **mobile UX** (tap-to-move, clear HUD, readable text)
  - A distinct **visual identity** (glam/noir mashup)
  - At least one **GenAI-assisted element** in the build pipeline or in-world content.
