# Meta Horizon 2025 — Devpost Addendum to Grok’s Guide (Nov 2025)

> This addendum captures **new/changed rules and clarifications** taken directly from the official Devpost pages for both Meta Horizon contests. It supplements the Grok master rules doc in Dropbox. (See our canonical source: “Meta Horizons 2025 rules from Grok.txt”.)

**Covers**
- **Contest A:** Meta Horizon Creator Competition — *Mobile Innovation* (Horizon Worlds; mobile-first)
- **Contest B:** Meta Horizon Start Developer Competition 2025 (Quest apps on Horizon OS)

---

## A. Creator Competition — Mobile Innovation (Horizon Worlds)

### What’s different vs. Grok
- **Category selection cap = 2.** Devpost allows selecting **up to two (2)** special categories on the submission form (not three).  
  Sources: Rules & FAQ (Devpost).
- **Judging weights are explicit and even (25% each):** *Technical Innovation*, *Visuals & Sound*, *Mobile Experience Design*, *Social Connectivity*.  
  Source: Devpost Judging section (Rules page).
- **Mobile interactivity is mandatory.** VR‑only worlds are **ineligible**; the world must include **interactive content for mobile** and be **public**.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Deliverables tightened:** public world URL; **≤3‑minute** public/unlisted video (YouTube/Vimeo); **200–300 words** written description; **English** (or English subtitles).  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Multiple entries allowed** if each entry is **unique**; you may enter as an **individual** and also as a member of **one (1) team**; a team must designate a **Representative**.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Community Favorite voting** is **MHCP‑verified only**; anti‑manipulation rules (bots/multi‑accounts/paid voting) apply.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **IP/Branding**: do not include brand logos or commercial artwork unless you hold rights; only licensed third‑party assets.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Post‑deadline edits**: changing your submission after the deadline may result in disqualification; keep your world accessible during judging.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Templates**: Sponsor may package certain entries as **templates** for community use.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.

### Submission checklist (Creator Competition)
- [ ] World is **newly published** during the contest window and **public**; owner is **MHCP** member.  
- [ ] Designed **mobile‑first** (portrait, tap‑to‑move); short 2–5 min loop; clear UI & audio on phones.  
- [ ] Uses at least **one GenAI** feature (Creator Assistant, Noesis UI, GenAI audio/texture).  
- [ ] **Select ≤2 categories** that we can realistically win.  
- [ ] **≤3‑minute** mobile demo video, **200–300 words** description.  
- [ ] **No edits** after deadline; keep the world accessible.

## B. Start Developer Competition — Quest / Horizon OS

### What’s different vs. Grok
- **New _or Updated_** projects are allowed; updates must be **significant** (e.g., **hand interactions**, **multiplayer**, **MR mode**, **UGC**). Cosmetic fixes / performance‑only / minor UI tweaks **do not** count.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Submission packaging** depends on tech:  
  - Unity / Unreal / Spatial / Native: upload **APK** to the **“Competition”** release channel in the Developer Dashboard.  
  - **Immersive Web SDK** (WebXR): provide a **hosted link** (e.g., GitHub Pages).  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Judging criteria (equally weighted):** *Innovation & Creativity*, *Experience Design*, *Technical Implementation* (**≥60 FPS** on Quest 3/3S), *Polish & Presentation*. **No modifications after the deadline**.  
  Source: Devpost Judging page.
- **Eligibility:** must be a **Start** member by submission time; **18+**; **excluded geos** include **Brazil**, **Quebec**, and OFAC‑sanctioned regions. Entrants may be individuals, **teams**, or **organizations**; multiple entries permitted if substantially different; **one team/organization per person**.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.
- **Tracks:** Entertainment, Lifestyle, Gaming → **Casual** or **Social**.  
  Source: Devpost Rules.

### Submission checklist (Start Developer)
- [ ] **Track chosen** (Entertainment / Lifestyle / Gaming: Casual or Social).  
- [ ] Build runs **≥60 FPS** on Quest 3/3S; includes **hand interactions**; experiment with **passthrough + AI** to target special awards.  
- [ ] If updating, document a **significant update** changelog in Devpost.  
- [ ] Submit **APK to “Competition”** release channel (or **WebXR link**); include **≤3‑min Quest demo** video and clear description.  
- [ ] Verify **Start** membership and **eligible region**; freeze builds after deadline.
